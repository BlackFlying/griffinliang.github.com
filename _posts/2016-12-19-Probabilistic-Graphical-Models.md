## Lecture One

### Flow of Probabilistic Influence

When can x influence y?

Yes: x->y; x<-y; x->w->y; x<-w<-y; x<-w->y.

No: x->w<-y (v-structure).

Active Trail: 

1) a trail has no v-structure 
2) any v-structure is observed and no other is observed.
